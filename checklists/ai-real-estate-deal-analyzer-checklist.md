# AI Real Estate Deal Analyzer Checklist

Use this checklist before spending serious time on a rental property candidate.

## Required inputs

- Property address
- Purchase price or asking price
- Estimated market rent
- Annual taxes
- Annual insurance
- HOA, if any
- Repair budget
- Down payment percentage
- Interest rate
- Loan term
- Vacancy assumption
- Maintenance assumption
- Property management assumption

## Core outputs

A useful deal analyzer should calculate or explain:

- Monthly cash flow
- Net operating income
- Cap rate
- Cash-on-cash return
- DSCR
- Break-even rent
- Repair risk
- Rent estimate confidence
- A-F deal grade
- Suggested next step

## Red flags

- Rent estimate is not supported by nearby comps
- Taxes are based on stale owner-occupied values
- Insurance is guessed too low
- Repairs are missing or vague
- Cash flow is only positive with optimistic assumptions
- The deal relies entirely on appreciation
- DSCR is below lender comfort thresholds

## Best use of AI

AI is best for:

- summarizing the deal clearly
- surfacing risk factors
- generating next-step recommendations
- turning analysis into a shareable report
- creating listing copy and flyer text

AI is not a substitute for:

- inspection
- local rent comps
- lender terms
- title review
- legal or tax advice
