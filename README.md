## Quick rental screening checklist

Before spending time on a rental candidate, check:

1. **Rent estimate** — does market rent support the price?
2. **Taxes and insurance** — are they current and realistic after purchase?
3. **Financing** — what payment does the actual down payment and rate create?
4. **Vacancy and maintenance** — do the numbers survive conservative assumptions?
5. **Monthly cash flow** — is the deal positive after debt service?
6. **Cap rate** — is the yield worth the risk?
7. **Cash-on-cash return** — does the cash invested earn enough?
8. **DSCR** — can income cover debt service?
9. **Repair risk** — can rehab scope erase the spread?
10. **Exit flexibility** — can the property work as rental, resale, or listing?
